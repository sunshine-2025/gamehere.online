About Escape Road

Escape Road puts you in the driver's seat of an intense urban adventure. Navigate through bustling city streets, avoid obstacles, and perfect your driving skills in this thrilling escape driving experience.

Game Controls

  • Arrow Keys or WASD - Control vehicle movement
  • Space Bar - Emergency brake
  • Shift - Boost speed (when available)
  • P - Pause game

Key Features

  • Dynamic city environments with realistic traffic
  • Multiple challenging levels and missions
  • Responsive vehicle controls
  • Progressive difficulty system
  • Various vehicles to unlock

Tips for Success

  • Master the timing of your turns
  • Watch out for traffic patterns
  • Use shortcuts to gain advantages
  • Maintain your vehicle's condition

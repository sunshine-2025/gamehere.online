About Music Now

Music Now is an interactive music creation game that lets you explore the world of music in a fun and engaging way. Create beautiful melodies, experiment with different instruments, and discover your musical creativity.

Game Controls

  • Mouse - Click to create and place notes
  • Keyboard - Use number keys to select different instruments
  • Space Bar - Play/Stop your composition
  • Delete - Remove notes

Key Features

  • Multiple instruments to choose from
  • Intuitive music creation interface
  • Real-time playback
  • Save and share your compositions
  • Learn basic music theory while playing

Tips for Success

  • Start with simple melodies and gradually add complexity
  • Experiment with different instrument combinations
  • Use the grid to maintain rhythm and timing
  • Try creating different moods with various note patterns
